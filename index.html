About Our School

Nyakato Boys Secondary School is one of the historic schools in Bukoba, Kagera. Established in 1919, the school provides quality education and promotes discipline, excellence, and leadership among students.

Vision and Mission

Vision

To be a leading secondary school that produces responsible, knowledgeable, and innovative students for the future.

Mission

To provide high-quality education through dedication, discipline, and teamwork, preparing students academically and morally.

Subject Combinations

  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)
  • PGM (Physics, Geography, Mathematics)
  • PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • CBG (Chemistry, Biology, Geography)
  • HKL (History, Kiswahili, Language)
  • HGK (History, Geography, Kiswahili)
  • HGE (History, Geography, Economics)

Classes

The school offers Advanced Level classes with well-equipped classrooms and experienced teachers to support all subject combinations.
